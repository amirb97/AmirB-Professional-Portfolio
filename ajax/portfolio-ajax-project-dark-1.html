Su Casa

Project Description:

MERN Stack site for a local restaurant, with JWT user authentication. The Su Casa website features an about page, food and drink menus, a gallery of dishes, drinks, and the restaurant itself, and an events page, where users can leave comments and create discussion about Su Casa events.
